This is a potato dish recipe I came up with on a very hungrily-faithful day. I was craving irish potatoes and I was tired of the regular fried/boiled potatoes and eggs. My taste-buds wanted something different, so I thought, why not make a potato dish with veggies! It was an Ah-ha moment and I could already imagine how it would taste. And trust me, it did taste delicious!

So, I have decided to give back to humanity by sharing my potato dish recipe with y’all. Salivate!

Preparation time: 20mins

Cook time: 15mins

Feeds: 2 people

Ingredients Measurements
Irish potatoes 10 small to medium pieces
Green Bell Peppers 1 big size
Red Bell peppers 1 big size
Yellow Bell peppers 1 big size
Chilli Peppers 5 pieces
Carrots 2 big size
Sausages 3 pieces
Eggs 2 eggs
Oyster/Soy Sauce 5 tablespoons
Grounded Pepper 1 tablespoons
Vegetable oil 5 tablespoons
Seasoning cubes 1 cube
Salt To taste

Directions

  1. Peel your potatoes and dice into desired shape.
  2. Blanch your diced veggies and sausages by soaking in hot water for 2 mins, drain and set aside.
  3. Boil your dices potatoes till almost ready, drain of excess oil and set aside.
  4. Break your eggs into a plate, season, fry till almost ready, drain of excess oil.
  5. In a pan, heat up your vegetable oil or any preferred cooking oil on a low heat.
  6. Add your potato and stir fry for 5 minutes.
  7. Add your veggies and sausages, stir fry for another 5 minutes.
  8. Add your oyster/soy sauce, grounded pepper, seasoning cubes, salt and stir fry for another 5 minutes.
  9. Lastly, add your scrambled eggs, stir fry for two minutes.
  10. Voila! Food is ready!
